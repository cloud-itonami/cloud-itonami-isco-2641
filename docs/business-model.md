## Customer

- readers (direct serialization)
- independent publishers and small presses
- serialization platforms
- businesses commissioning long-form writing

## Offer

- serialized fiction (episode cadence, worldview continuity)
- commissioned writing and editing
- manuscript preparation and proof fulfilment
- rights licensing and reprint management

## Revenue

- serialization subscription
- per-work / per-episode fee
- rights and reprint royalties

## Trust Controls

- no unlicensed source or plagiarized passage without governor gate
- publishing-rights overrides require human sign-off
- unreleased manuscripts are never disclosed without consent
- manuscript and rights records are auditable, not editable
